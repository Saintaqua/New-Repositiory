Raven, Countess, Aaliyah, Kennedy,and Mahaogany are 5 girls who are fated with the role of becoming magical girls known as maidens. Each girl possess a power unique to them. As maidens it is their job to defeat monsters known as the Saul's and cleanse the Ginkgo tree to protect the world.
