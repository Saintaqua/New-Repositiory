Imani Reign

Niyah Ilewood

La'Niyah "Niyah" Ilewood is one of the members of "The Circle", which is the friend group that consists of the Meraki's Sin team along with Imani and Sierra. Niyah has the seen the girls around school but did not become friends with them until this year when they formed The Circle. Niyah is fascinated with health related topics and one day wants to save lives. Niyah is seen around school selling snacks and is known as the "Human Vending Machine".

Wisdom Greenleaf

Wisdom Greenleaf is Countess' step-sister. When their mother decides to move them to Silver Haven, She faces the same issues as Countess such as being the new girl and finding her own identity. Though her and her sister are close, she tries to make her experience at Silver Haven her own, rather than relying on her sister to make friends. It seems at though she has made her own group of friends and has broken the narrative of being the vice principal's daughter.

Vice Principal Greenleaf

Given the oppurtunity to the move to a new area for a better position, Ms.Greenleaf moves her and her daughters to Silver Haven. Vice Principal Greenleaf serves as the Principal's right hand woman and hopes that her daughters will appreciate their new life. As a mother, their old life didn't provide them to greatest oppurtunities, but now she can do better to provide for her daughters.

Gin

Gin is a being created by the Ginkgo tree to help and guide the maidens with their new-found powers. Though they are neither male or female, they take on the form of a woman. The girls decide to name them Gin since Gin is short for "Ginkgo". Much is not known about Gin, but as the girls interact with them, the more you get to see their personality.
