Raven Valentine

Raven Valentine is a 15 year old, "2nd year" at Silver Haven Preparatory Academy/Insititute. On the first day of school when she meets the others, she discovers that would soon become a protector known as a Maiden. Raven was given the powers to control time and as the time maiden, she serves as the leader of the team known as "Meraki's Sin". Her artistic ambitions and creative spirit allows her to creatively tap into her powers since she is not linked to a season. Raven is able to stop, speed up, or slow down certain objects in time but she is not able to travel or alter time itself.

Countess Greenleaf

Countess Greenleaf is a 15 year old, "2nd year" at Silver Haven Preparatory Academy/Insititute. She transfers to the school with her step sister Wisdom and her step mother who is now the vice principal. As the new girl at Silver Haven, she tries to adjust and learn the culture around the boarding school and establish her own identity. On the first day she meets Raven, who used to be the new girl last year. As a maiden, Countess is linked to the season of Summer which represents fire but she is only able to control gunpowder.

Aaliyah Cresston

Aaliyah Cresston is a 16 year old, "2nd year" at Silver Haven Preparatory Academy/Insititute. On the first day of school, she meets the others girls and becomes a maiden. Aaliyah someday wants to become student council president and make a change at her school, but her duties as a maiden may stand in her way. As a maiden, Aaliyah is linked to the season of Winter which represents ice but she is only able to control water and change its state of matter.

Kennedy Simmons

Kennedy Simmons is a 16 year old, "2nd year" at Silver Haven Preparatory Academy/Insititute. On the first day of school, she meets the others girls and becomes a maiden. Kennedy is in multiple clubs and is naturally humorous. As a maiden, Kennedy is linked to the season of Spring the represents air and she controls air through the use of gomoku pieces that resemble the characterstics of chess pieces. Kennedy's powers grows gradually and she does not have access to all of the gomoku pieces when she is first given her powers.

Mahogany Nightingale

Mahogany is a 15 year old, "2nd year" at Silver Haven Preparatory Academy/Insititute. On the first day of school, she meets the others girls and becomes a maiden. Mahogany is on the cheer team, "The Silver Haven Havenettes". As a maiden, Mahogany is linked to the season of Autumn which represents the earth but she can only control sand.
